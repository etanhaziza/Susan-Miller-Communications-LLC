About Susan

Professional Highlights

In addition to being a longtime reporter, columnist and editor, Susan is a successful grant writer and has been a presenter of journalism training sessions at the New Jersey Press Association. She has also participated in media forums held by governors of New Jersey, served as co-chair on a Diversity, Inclusion and Global committee for a nonprofit, and was keynote speaker at the Philadelphia Writers Conference.

Awards and Nominations

Throughout her journalism career, Susan has been the recipient of many prestigious writing/editing/design awards and recognitions, and was nominated for an award for excellence in Cardiovascular Reporting by the American Heart Association.

Community Leadership Roles

Active in her community, Susan served as President of Medford Sunrise Rotary, was on the board of trustees for the South Jersey Pops Orchestra and served as President of the Shawnee High School Band Parents Association. She’s also done public image work for various events, including Medford’s annual Dickens Festival and local jazz band performances.
